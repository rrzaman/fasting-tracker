# 011 - Fast 16th of Dhul Hijjah as Ayyam al-Bid Compensation

**Date:** 2026-04-22

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The 13th of every Hijri month is an Ayyam al-Bid (White Day) recommended for fasting. In Dhul Hijjah, the 13th is Ayyam al-Tashreeq when fasting is prohibited. The 14th and 15th remain valid Ayyam al-Bid days.

## Decision

Fast the 16th of Dhul Hijjah as a personal compensatory practice to observe three white days that month. Acceptable according to some Islamic scholars

## Consequences

- Personal practice, not universally prescribed, for those who regularly observe Ayyam al-Bid fasting
- Notification logic extended to include 16th of Dhul Hijjah
- Receive notification on the 13th for fasting on the 14th-16th.
